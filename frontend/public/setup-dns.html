Checking status…

Step 1 — Open your dashboards

Open Squarespace Domains Open Resend Domains

In Squarespace: Domains → connectintel.net → DNS Settings

Step 2 — Edit root SPF (one TXT at @)

Replace the existing Google SPF value with this (copy):

v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com include:amazonses.com ~all

Step 3 — Add 3 custom records

TypeHostValue

Step 4 — Verify in Resend

  1. In Resend, open connectintel.net
  2. Click Verify DNS records
  3. Wait until status is Verified
