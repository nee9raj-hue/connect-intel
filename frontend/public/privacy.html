Connect Intel — B2B CRM and AI prospect search for sales teams.

Last updated: May 2026

What we collect

  • Account data — When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture to create and secure your account.
  • CRM data — Leads, contacts, companies, pipeline stages, notes, tasks, meetings, and email activity you save in the product.
  • Search data — Search queries and filters you run in AI prospect search.

How we use Google user data

Connect Intel requests Google OAuth scopes only when you explicitly connect your work Gmail:

  • gmail.send — To send CRM emails you compose in Connect Intel from your own work mailbox (e.g. sales@yourcompany.com). We do not send email without your action.
  • gmail.readonly (optional) — To sync replies between your mailbox and leads already in your CRM pipeline. We query Gmail only for threads involving lead email addresses you are working on.
  • email / profile — To identify which Google account you connected and display your connected mailbox in settings.

We do not sell Google user data. We do not use Gmail data for advertising. We do not read unrelated personal email.

Google API Services User Data Policy

Connect Intel’s use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. In summary:

  • We use Gmail data only to provide user-facing CRM email features (send, optional reply sync).
  • We do not transfer Gmail data to third parties except as needed to operate the service (hosting) or when required by law.
  • We do not use Gmail data for ads or to build unrelated user profiles.
  • Humans do not read Gmail content except when necessary for security, support with your consent, or legal compliance.

Data storage and retention

CRM and account data are stored on secure cloud infrastructure (Vercel, Supabase). Gmail OAuth refresh tokens are stored encrypted at rest and used only to send mail or sync threads you request. You may disconnect Gmail at any time by revoking access at Google Account permissions.

How we use data

To provide B2B CRM features, team collaboration, AI prospect search, and optional email sending from your connected work Gmail.

Third parties

We use hosting (Vercel), database (Supabase), Google Gmail API (when you connect work Gmail), and optional email delivery providers as configured.

Your choices

  • Disconnect work Gmail in Google Account settings or contact support to remove tokens.
  • Request account deletion by emailing invite@connectintel.net.
