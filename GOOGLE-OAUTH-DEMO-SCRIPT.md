# Demo video script for Google OAuth verification

**Length:** 2–3 minutes  
**Format:** Unlisted YouTube, 1080p screen recording with voice or captions  
**URL to show in browser:** https://connectintel.net

---

## 1. Intro (15 sec)

“This is Connect Intel, a B2B CRM. Sales reps sign in with Google and connect their work Gmail to send email to leads from their own mailbox.”

## 2. Sign in (20 sec)

- Open https://connectintel.net
- Click sign in with Google
- Complete Google sign-in (show consent for basic profile — not gmail yet)

## 3. Connect work Gmail (45 sec)

- After onboarding, show **Connect your work email** prompt (or sidebar **Work email**)
- Click **Connect work email**
- Show Google OAuth consent including **gmail.send** (and gmail.readonly if requested)
- Approve → return to app → “Work email connected” with mailbox shown

## 4. Send CRM email (40 sec)

- Open **Pipeline** → open a lead with email
- **Email** tab → compose subject/body → **Send**
- Briefly show success / activity logged on lead

## 5. Marketing send (30 sec, optional)

- **Marketing → Campaigns** → small draft list → **Start campaign**
- Show progress or success (few recipients is fine)

## 6. Privacy (15 sec)

- Open https://connectintel.net/privacy.html
- Scroll to **How we use Google user data** — mention no ads, no sale of data, disconnect via Google Account permissions

## 7. Close (10 sec)

“Data is only used to provide CRM email features the user initiates. Thank you.”
