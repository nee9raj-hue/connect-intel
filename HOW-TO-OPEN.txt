CONNECT INTEL — HOW TO OPEN THE APP
====================================

STEP 1 — Double-click:  index.html
        (Opens a launcher page with instructions)

STEP 2 — Best way on Mac:
        Double-click:  OPEN CONNECT INTEL.command

        → Browser opens the full app at http://localhost:4173
        → Keep the terminal window open while using the app
        → Press Ctrl+C in terminal to stop

WHY NOT JUST index.html FOR THE FULL APP?
Browsers block modern apps opened as plain files (security).
The .command file starts a tiny local server — this always works.

ALTERNATIVE (TERMINAL)
----------------------
cd "/Users/apple/Downloads/Connect intel"
npx serve .
Open the localhost link in your browser.

FILES
-----
index.html  = launcher (small, always opens)
app.html    = full app (may be blank if opened as file — use .command)
OPEN CONNECT INTEL.command = recommended on Mac
